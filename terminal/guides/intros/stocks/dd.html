Due Diligence

The Due Diligence submenu, located within the Stocks menu, is primarily for supplementing fundamental analysis with information such as analyst ratings and price targets over time, earnings estimates, SEC filings and business-to-business customers and suppliers. Entering the submenu requires having a ticker loaded from the Stocks menu. With a stock selected, type dd into the command line and press enter.
